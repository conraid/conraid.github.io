About

Brief summary about me

I am a Webmaster, based in Leghorn (Italy), specializing in Web Design and Development.

I try to build clean, usable and accessible websites with the latest W3C standards.

In particular I develop themes for WordPress, with HTML5/CSS3/BootStrap.

I use only free software, especially Slackware Linux. Utilizzo solo free software, in particolare Slackware Linux, for which I manage a repository. You can see my opensource projects on GitHub.

I have been doing all this and much more since 1996.
